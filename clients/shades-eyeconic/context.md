# Shades Eyeconic — Working Memory

## Project context
Shades Eyeconic is a US-focused DTC sunglasses / eyewear brand. Ecommerce-first, no physical retail. Positioning target: the $50–$90 fashion DTC tier between Warby Parker ($95+) and EyeBuyDirect (sub-$35).

## Owner
- Andy (andres.belmonte@designereyes.com)

## Key reference files
- `memory/competitors.md` — full US eyewear competitor intelligence (10 competitors, April 2026)
- Source: `Analysis Competitors SE.docx` (uploaded April 23, 2026)

## Strategic priorities (from April 2026 competitive intel)
1. Own the $50–$90 fashion DTC positioning (gap between Warby Parker and EyeBuyDirect).
2. Build an SEO content moat vs. EyeBuyDirect & Clearly (long-tail queries, 2–3 editorial posts/month).
3. Launch home try-on or AR virtual try-on (Warby Parker benchmark).
4. Activate TikTok Shop before competitors in this tier do.
5. Build trust signals (brand, UGC, creators, origin story) to beat GlassesUSA's discount-driven model.

## Primary threats
- **Sunglass Hut** — Luxottica-owned, mall + airport monopoly, Google Shopping dominance.
- **Warby Parker** — benchmark DTC, home try-on + AR.
- **EyeBuyDirect / Zenni** — budget-tier SEO and price-floor dominance ($6–$22 entry).
- **GlassesUSA / Clearly** — aggressive designer discounting (up to 70% off).

## SWOT snapshot
- **Strengths:** full DTC margins, ownable aesthetic identity, agility, owned customer data.
- **Weaknesses:** no physical try-on, low brand awareness, SEO DA gap, no home try-on.
- **Opportunities:** unclaimed $50–$90 fashion DTC tier, TikTok Shop, SEO content gaps, micro-influencer affiliates.
- **Threats:** GlassesUSA/Clearly discounting, EyeBuyDirect/Zenni budget SEO, Sunglass Hut Google Shopping, Warby Parker catalog expansion.
